# kaiui-toast 

**This component is automatically integrated as a Mixin. Just use**

`this.showToast("I'm a Toast")`

**in your components.** 

- **author** - Sebastian Baar 
- **license** - MIT 

## data 

- `title` 

  The Title
  ***String*** (*required*)
  `default: ''` 

**initial value:** `''` 

- `time` 

  The Time in ms
  ***Number*** (*optional*)
  `default: 2500` 

**initial value:** `2500` 

## methods 

- `show(title, time)`
